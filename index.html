During this initial stage, the focus is on understanding the mentee's current knowledge, experience level and specific needs:

  • Bi-weekly 1-on-1 mentorship sesions to assess the mentee's understanding of basic trading concepts.
  • Access to an exclusive trading community for networking and sharing insights.
  • Receive trade alerts and market analysis to familiarize with market dynamics.
  • Begin development of a personalized trading plan tailored to the mentee's risk tolerance and financial goals.

Stage 2: Strategy Development and Customization

Building on the foundational knowlede, this stae emphasizes the development of effective trading strategies alligned with mentee's challenges and current skill level:

  • Daily mentorship sessions focusing on advanced trade strategies and techniques
  • Personalized coaching to address specific challenges and refine trading skills
  • Detailed feedback on trading performance and decision-making process
  • Dive deeper into market psychology and risk management strategies tailored to the mentee's evolving needs.
  • Continued refinement and customization of the trading plan to optimize perfomance and adapt to changing market conditions

In the final stage, the focus shifts to monitoring progress and ensuring sustained growth and improvement:

  • Regular session to review and reinforce learning outcomes, assessing the mentee's development
  • Priority access to new trading opportunities and exclusive insights.
